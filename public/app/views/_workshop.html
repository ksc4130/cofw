All-day Workshop with

Larry Brooks


Author of Story Fix and Story Engineering

Location: The Courtyard Marriott, 2350 Westbest Drive, Columbus

  2. The Beautiful Collision of Concept and Premise
  3. Dramatic Arc as the Key to Everything (including character)
  4. When Every Month is NanoWriMo
