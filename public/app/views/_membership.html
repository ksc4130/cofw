COFW is a chapter of Romance Writers of America. Those seeking membership in COFW must be members of Romance Writers of America. COFW Membership is $25 per year. There is a $5 late fee for renewing members who pay after January 31. For questions regarding membership to COFW, please contact cofwtreasurer@gmail.com. Also, dues are not deductible as a charitable contribution but may qualify as a business expense.
If you have any questions about COFW before joining, please feel free to contact us.
